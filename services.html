Our Services

Full Dentures

Full dentures (complete dentures) replace all of your natural teeth.

These dentures will be fitted to your top or bottom gum line and will be held in place by suction.
One of the benefits of our technology is our customers very rarely require any type of oral adhesive to secure their dentures. Our fittings are done with accuracy and precision to ensure there is no need for adhesive and you are left with comfortable fitting dentures.

For more information on Full Dentures please contact us.

Partial Dentures

Partial dentures replace a cluster of missing teeth.

Partial dentures are a great solution for replacing multiple teeth. They are a great option when the surrounding natural teeth are not strong enough for other dental sturctures, such as birdges. Partial dentures are also a great solution should you be missing more than one tooth.

Partial dentures are fastened to nearby teeth and are fitted to the part of the gum on which they will sit. They dentures are not permanently fastened and can be taken out as needed.

For more information on Partial Dentures please contact us.

Dental Implants

Denture implants can be used to replace an individual, cluster or full set of teeth.

Partial dentures are fastened to nearby teeth and are fitted to the part of the gum on which they will sit. They dentures are not permanently fastened and can be taken out as needed.
